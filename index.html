Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy informs you about how your data is collected, used, and protected within the scope of the Maneviyat İklimi mobile application. Your security is important to us; therefore, the data you share with us is protected with sensitivity.

1. Processing, Storage, and Deletion of Data

Your data is processed in accordance with the law and honesty rules, for legitimate purposes, and limited to the purpose for which they are processed. Necessary measures are taken to ensure that your data is kept accurate and up to date.

Your data, which is stored for the period stipulated in the relevant legislation or required for the purpose for which they are processed, is deleted or destroyed at the end of this period.

3. Collected Data

The data collected by this application may include:

  • User-Generated Content: The questions you submit through the application.
  • Device Information: Device model and operating system version for compatibility.
  • Push Notification Tokens (FCM Token): A unique, anonymous string of characters assigned to your device. This does not contain personal identifiable information (PII).

4. Purposes of Data Processing

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Publishing your spiritual and religious questions to be answered by administrators.
  • Push Notifications: Using your FCM Token to send you a notification solely when your specific question has been answered, or for general app announcements (e.g., daily hadith or quotes).
  • Improving service quality, communication, and overall security.

5. Transfer of Data

Your data will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except for legal obligations. However, it may be shared with our service providers (e.g., Google Firebase) strictly to provide the application's core functionalities, such as database storage and cloud messaging.

6. Legal Rights

You have the right to learn whether your data is processed, request correction of incomplete data, or request the deletion of your data. For these requests, contact devmalidemir@gmail.com.

7. Services Used

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: Used as a database to securely store questions and answers.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Used exclusively to deliver push notifications to your device.

8. Consent and Validity

This Privacy Policy entered into force on 26.04.2026 and is updated when necessary.
